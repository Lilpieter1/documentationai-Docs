## Invite Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Go to the project's page from your Nousu dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Collaborators'">
    Find the 'Collaborators' tab in the top menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add collaborator">
    Enter your teammate's email and click "Invite". They'll receive an email to join your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
