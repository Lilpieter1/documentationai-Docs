---
title: Project Not Showing Up
description: Resolve missing projects in your Nousu dashboard.
---

## Symptoms

- Recently created project doesn't appear
- Can't find project in dashboard search

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh dashboard">
    Click the browser refresh or use F5. Projects may take a few seconds to sync.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log out and back in">
    Log out of Nousu and sign in again to trigger a fresh sync.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear your cache and cookies, then reload Nousu.
  </Step>
</Steps>
