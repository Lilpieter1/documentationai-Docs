## Quick Start

Get Nousu running on your Shopify store in four steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your account at `https://nousu.nl/signup`. No credit card needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install App" icon="download">
    Add the Nousu app from the Shopify App Store.
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="Shopify CLI,npx">
      ```bash
      shopify app install nousu-support
      ```
      ```bash
      npx @shopify/create-app@latest nousu-integration
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure AI" icon="settings">
    Connect your store and train Nousu on your products via the dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch" icon="rocket">
    Enable live chat. Monitor performance in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
